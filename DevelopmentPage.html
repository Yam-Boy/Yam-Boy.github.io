My Personal Development Journey

The last five years of my life consists of me entering 4th form of my high school, Bridgeport high,
to the continuation of my university life as a second year student at The University of Technology.
During my adolescent years I spent a lot of time contemplating life and ruminating on the thought
of self-improvement, though this was more so in an effort to be acknowledged by my peers rather
than any benefits I would expereince after reaping the fruits of my labor, labors which included.

  • Reading
  • Meditation
  • Creating Art
  • Routine Excercise
This period of improving myself allowed me to develop my self-discipline to higher degree than it
was previously. Secondly my creativity as an individual was also bolstered due to the struggle of
having to create new interesting pieces of art consistently. Finally, the amount new content that I
was reading increased my vocabulary, comprehensive ability, developed my critical thinking skills
and decision-making skills. With all this in consideration.I can confidently say
that my strengths are:
  • My Intellect
  • My Discipline
  • My Creativity

12 Pillars

4: Achieve Your Goals

This chapter of the book focuses on the importance of goal setting in an individuals life
in order to achieve their goals. If you set a goal within reason and work earnestly to
achieve it you will come to realise that you are able to work more effciently towards it
as you now have a driving force pushing you to achieve what it is you set out to do. With
this, figuring out what it is you want to achieve is one of the most important aspects to
becoming successful.

5: The Proper Use of Time

On the road to success, each step taken is very important as they are the building blocks
for your next step forward. Each step leads into the other, much like how each moment
spent on a task contributes to your future success.
With this in mind, we must ensure that we use our time efficiently and sieze the day at
every oppurtunity as what we do now determines what we can achieve later on. We must
then take it upon ourselves to determine the most effective way forward and press forward
on that path.

6: Surround Yourself with the Best People

Proverbs 27:17 proclaims, "Iron sharpeneth iron; so a man sharpeneth the countenance of his friend."
This statement received from the bible, is used to educate one's self on the value of having a good
social group. You must ensure that you surround yourself with people who will test, challenge and
push you to higher heights.
"No man is an island; No man stands alone. Each man's joy is a joy to me; Each man's grief is my own."
, this verse taken from a christian hymn, shows us the importance of relationships, but above that are
relationships that exist to improve both parties involved.

7: Be A Life-long Learner

The benefits of "Formal Education" cannot be denounced as it serves to educate the larger population,
but many individuals neglect the pursuit of educating themselves outside of traditional schooling, thereafter
its completion. "Self-Education" is necessary for the continuous development of an individual physically,
emotionally, intellectual and in regards to their maturation. Thus in a effort to avoid stagnation in life
post formal education, one should: read, explore, discuss, seek counsel and learn by learn by whatever
means presented to you.

Self-Reflection

Having read this I consider that in some aspects of my life I fall short.
I am not particlarly proficient at planning for the achievement of goals.
I tend to procrastinate and not effeciently use my time as wisely as I could
My peer group may not be the best at pushing me to achieving accolades greater
than I have previously.
But, I do find my self resonating with the idea of life-long learning and the
pursuit of knowledge used to most benefits one's life.
However, scrutinizing every aspect of my life to find what does or does not work
is not the optimal way to live.
I am thankful for what I have and what I am capable of and I will strive achieve
more, than what I can right now, keeping in mind the lessons from this book.
